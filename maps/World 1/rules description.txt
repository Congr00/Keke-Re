Basic rules:
1. get to flag to win (you know what is the flag on spotting it)
2. you have limited vision (9x9?)
3. on clicking a button you are informed of what position or group changed
4. every level has a hard limit of moves you can make until you lose
5. death resets the entire level, but not the move counter
6. the game will never troll you by saying it did something when it actually didnt do anything

0 - group of floor
1 - group of default wall
Available object rules:
- win condition
- blocks vision
- unpassable
- kills you on contact

Available button rules:
- transform a group of objects into another
- change object rules for a single group
